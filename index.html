Filter Policy

This list has no economic or political motivation. No donations sought. No opinions censored.

The primary goal of this list is to block the following on Swedish websites:

Ads

  • Banners
  • Text/CSS ads
  • Video/Audio ads
  • Sponsored content
  • Ad scripts
  • Ad placeholders
  • Affiliate links
  • Other types of commercial advertising

Tracking

  • Analytics/telemetry
  • Tracking pixels
  • Query parameters
  • Fingerprinting
  • Beacons
  • Hit counters
  • Adblock tracking
  • Other types of tracking

Annoyances & bloat

  • Anti-adblock
  • Newsletter popups
  • Campaign popups
  • Cookie messages1
  • Useless effects (for example xmas snow)
  • Cryptomining scripts
  • Anything that prevents the user from using their devices or browser as intended
  • Any unnecessary interruptions

Scams & badware

  • Malicious scripts
  • Malicious URLs
  • Malware
  • Fake shops & frauds
  • Fake paywalls2

1 Cookie messages will only be blocked if they don't appear to break anything.

2 A fake paywall is one that can be bypassed easily. It is not considered protected content if it's already available on the front-end without user verification.

Consideration cannot always be given to things that ordinary visitors do not have access to. (For example, administrative interfaces and the like.)
