Please note that some fixes in this list may not work correctly if you have multiple content blocker extensions activated. It is recommended to only use uBlock Origin and deactivate or uninstall other blockers such as Adblock Plus, Ghostery, etc.

uBlock Origin Lite is partly supported by this list. I won't fix any issues that may require me to allowlist anything for specific individuals who use that extension through a group policy. If you need something allowlisted you should either deactivate the list or switch the users to Firefox with the full uBlock Origin extension that allows you to create your own allowlists.

Filter Policy

This list has no economic or political motivation. No donations sought. No opinions censored.

The primary goal of this list is to block the following on Swedish websites:

Ads

  • Banners
  • Text/CSS ads
  • Video/Audio ads
  • Sponsored content
  • Ad scripts
  • Ad placeholders
  • Affiliate links
  • Other types of commercial advertising

Tracking

  • Analytics/telemetry
  • Tracking pixels
  • Query parameters
  • Fingerprinting
  • Beacons
  • Hit counters
  • Adblock tracking
  • Other types of tracking

Annoyances & bloat

  • Anti-adblock
  • Newsletter popups
  • Campaign popups
  • Cookie messages1
  • Useless effects (for example xmas snow)
  • Cryptomining scripts
  • Anything that prevents the user from using their devices or browser as intended
  • Any unnecessary interruptions

Scams & badware

  • Malicious scripts
  • Malicious URLs
  • Malware
  • Fake shops & frauds
  • Fake paywalls2

1 Cookie messages will only be blocked if they don't appear to break anything.

2 A fake paywall is one that can be bypassed easily. It is not considered protected content if it's already available on the front-end without user verification.

Consideration cannot always be given to things that ordinary visitors do not have access to. (For example, administrative interfaces and the like.)
